How Important is Someone to You?

Introduction: This test helps you measure how important someone is in your life. Answer the questions honestly, and at the end you’ll receive a personalized result that reflects your true feelings.

Preliminary Question:

Please select the nature of your relationship with this person:



Test Questions - For Friends

Section 1: Thought & Attention

1. How often do you think about this friend daily?






2. If something exciting happens, how likely are you to tell them first?






3. Scenario: You are busy with work/studies, and this friend reaches out to chat. How do you respond?





Section 2: Emotional Impact

4. If they are upset, how does it affect you?






5. If they were to move away, how would you feel?






6. Scenario: Your friend cancels plans at the last minute. How do you react?





Section 3: Priority & Support

7. If they needed your help urgently, how likely would you be to drop everything?






8. Do you prioritize spending time with them over other commitments?






9. Scenario: You make plans with a friend, but something better comes up. What do you do?





Section 4: Presence & Absence

10. How do you feel when they don’t respond to your messages for a long time?






11. How would you feel if they were no longer part of your life?






Test Questions - For Romantic Partners

Section 1: Thought & Attention

1. How often do you think about your partner daily?






2. When you experience strong emotions, how often do you turn to them for support?






3. Scenario: You see a romantic message from your partner but are preoccupied. What do you do?





Section 2: Emotional Impact

4. How much does their happiness affect your own?






5. If your relationship ended, how would you feel?






6. Scenario: Your partner forgets an important date. How do you handle it?





Section 3: Priority & Support

7. If they needed emotional support, how likely are you to prioritize them over everything else?






8. How much do you value quality time with them over other responsibilities?






9. Scenario: You are out with friends, and your partner calls needing to talk. What do you do?





Section 4: Presence & Absence

10. How do you feel when they don’t communicate with you for an extended period?






11. If they suddenly disappeared from your life, how would you feel?
